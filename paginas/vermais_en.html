↪ The Anhumas Abyss has a unique beauty: a partially submerged cave, a crystalline lake and a forest of limestone cones – featuring the largest cones ever recorded in world literature. It is the purest state of nature.

↪ By visiting this incredible setting, you understand in practice the greatness of nature and time and our human frailty in the face of it all.

↪ Take beautiful photos of the Abyss Anhumas; Enjoy the look of the floating platform; Take a guided tour by inflatable boat, and learn more about the cave and its formation; Experience floating with Neoprene clothing; Dive into the lake of crystal clear waters.
